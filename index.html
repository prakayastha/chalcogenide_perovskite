Chalcogenide perovskites

This is a website showing results from an experimental + computational collaborative project on chalcogenide perovskites conduted at Northumbria University.
Currently, this is website constains results from DFT calculations on the BaZrS3 perovskite and related materials that could form during the synthesis process.
The contents of this website were made to assist in a poster presented at the IOP Advances in Materials conference.
